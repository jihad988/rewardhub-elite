Our Transparency Promise

We believe in complete transparency about how our reward platform operates.

Our rewards are funded through partnerships with market research companies and brands looking for consumer insights. When you complete qualified activities (surveys, product trials, etc.), these partners compensate us, allowing us to share rewards with you. We only work with established, reputable companies.

This business model ensures that we can continue offering high-value rewards without charging our members any fees.

All rewards are subject to verification to ensure completed activities meet partner requirements. This typically involves:

  • Review of activity completion time
  • Validation of response quality
  • Anti-fraud checks
  • Partner confirmation

This process typically takes 1-2 business days after activity completion. We notify you immediately if any issues are found.

Requirements vary by offer but may include:

  • Completing surveys (2-3 minutes)
  • Trying free trial products (cancel anytime)
  • Participating in focus groups
  • Providing product feedback

All requirements are clearly disclosed before you begin each offer. We never require credit card information for free reward opportunities.

We take your privacy seriously. Here's how we protect your information:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transfers
  • Strict data access controls
  • Never sell your personal information
  • Compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations

You can review our complete Privacy Policy for more details.
